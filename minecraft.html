How to Play:

To begin a new game, either click new game or simply start playing!

To load and save games click on the corrosponding buttons accordingly.

All the tools can be located conveniently to the right of the board.

To mine the grass and the dirt, you need to use the shovel.

To mine the trees, use the axe.

To mine the rocks, use the pickaxe.

To build, click on the inventory and set it on a logical tile.
